AI Learns To Race Hurdles

My fourth and final released project as an AIWarehouse employee.

This was the fourth and final released project for AIWarehouse that I worked on. While the majority of the work was done between Soccer and Red Light Green Light, it was released last. It was another project where a lot of creative freedom and design decisions were delegated to me, and it was the first project where I was entirely responsible for gathering of all footage.

Unfortunately, this ended up being the last video created for AIWarehouse. While other projects were in various stages of development, the company ultimately wasn’t profitable enough to continue and was shut down following the release of this video.

I really enjoyed my time at AIWarehouse, and I learned a lot about working in a game development team. It gave me a strong foundation working around reinforcement learning workflows and production constraints.

Contributions

  • Built AI training environments.
  • Animated sponsor segment & intro.
  • Implemented win scenario and end sequence.
  • Built a custom camera system to record multiple angles with smooth dolly movement.
  • Created a hurdle-height randomizer tool to improve training variety.
  • Implemented sound effects for agent actions and environmental triggers.
  • Adapted camera follow logic for non-camera objects to smoothly follow the lead agent.
  • Worked alongside AI developers, lead developer, and producers to keep scope on track.
  • Recorded and iterated on gameplay footage multiple times to capture the most interesting behaviors.

GAME JAM Blue Ocean Jam

Blue Ocean Rising Tide Challenge #1

Chronocase Noir (Working Title) is a detective mystery game where you are trapped in a time loop whilst embroiled in investigating a string of murders.

Chronocase: Noir is a game I have been wanting to make for years. A detective story stuck in a time loop, with a classic Film Noir aesthetic to bring it all together. I worked alongside 2 teammates: Chloe the narrative designer, and Jacob, the 3D artist. I handled the gameplay, interaction elements, game design and more.

This is a passion project that if I ever get the time or funding to make, I would love to bring to life.

Contributions

  • Handled Player Locomotion System
  • Implemented Player Camera System
  • Implemented Player Interaction System
  • Implemented Cork Evidence Board System
  • Implemented Sound FX/Footsteps System
  • Created most early assets, before eventually replacing them with higher quality ones.
  • Worked alongside Chloe to ensure the narrative and gameplay were cohesive and engaging.
  • Worked alongside Jacob to ensure the 3D assets were cohesive and engaging.
  • Recorded, Edited, and Compiled all gameplay footage for the project.
