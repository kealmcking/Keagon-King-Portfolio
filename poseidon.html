Game Info

Poseidon is Not Calm is a city builder where you have to build a city on a remote island. You must collect resources, create buildings, and prepare for the inevitable storms. Your main objective is to survive the storms and create a thriving town.

This game was created for the Brackeys Game Jam 2024.2. I worked alongside a mostly new team, save one who I met making Sender: Arbitration for the GMTK Game Jam 2024. We decided to go big with a city builder, and I was tasked with the design of the UI, as well as the overall UX of the game. It was a fun experience, and I learned a lot about the importance of UX in a game, as well as working with new people. While we weren't able to implement everything we wanted, I still feel like we made a fun game in the short time frame we had.
