Game Info

Combat-Ready NPC AI Sandbox is a gameplay AI systems project built in Unreal Engine 5 using C++, focused on designing believable and scalable NPC behavior for combat-driven games. Rather than shipping a full game, the project explores how perception, memory, and decision-making can be structured into a clean, extensible AI architecture. Proven locomotion and animation systems are reused so development effort remains centered on AI behavior and system design.

The AI framework supports autonomous patrol, player detection through sight and sound, pursuit while line-of-sight is maintained, investigation of last known locations, and reasoned combat decisions based on distance, health, and internal state. Behavior is driven by a Behavior Tree and Blackboard, with AI Perception supplying sensory input and custom services managing short-term memory and state transitions. All behaviors are interruptible and reactive, allowing NPCs to respond immediately to changing conditions rather than relying on scripted sequences or fixed timers.

This project demonstrates a production-oriented approach to gameplay AI, emphasizing separation of concerns, declarative decision logic, and designer-tunable systems. The architecture is built to scale into more advanced combat reasoning, archetype-driven behavior, and debugging visualization without structural rewrites. The result is an AI sandbox that prioritizes clarity, debuggability, and long-term extensibility, reflecting how combat AI is developed and iterated on in professional game development environments.
