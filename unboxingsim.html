Game Info

Unboxing Simulator was made for the GMTK Game Jam 2022, alongside 2 of my friends. One focused on the art and animation, the other on integrating these into the world and setting up events, while I focused on the interacting of the in-world UI via the computer screen, as well as setting up the UI itself.

It was a very interesting project to work on, effectively taking the unboxing loop from CS:GO and making it a sort of horror game.
