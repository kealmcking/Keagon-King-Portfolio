Game Info

Battle Balls is a top-down momentum based speed game that combines Rounds with Beyblade in a unique ball physics system.

Players battle it out in an arena, trying to deal damage to their opponents by hitting them with their chosen weapon. If you die, you get an upgrade to help even the odds. I focused primarily on User Interface implementation and visual design.

The project was very entertaining to work on, and was started as a way to try and create our own self balancing auto battler. As huge fans of Rounds, we wanted to see if we could replicate the replayability and upgrade cycle of that game.

The game remains as a demo, but it was a very important learning experience for me. I learned a lot about UI design, and how to make a game feel fun to play, even when you're not in control of the ball.
