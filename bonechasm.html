Game Info

Bone Chasm is a first person horror game set in the wild west. You play as a man who is traveling across the desert, when he wakes up to find his horse dead and most of his supplies missing. He must delve into the nearby mine, tredge through the ghost town, and find a way to escape before the beast finds him.

This game was made for the SCREAM JAM 2021. I worked alongside a friend on this one, and the responsibilities were evenly split into programming for me, and art direction/game design for him. All of the gameplay, including the enemy AI, player movement, weaponry, and interaction system was developed by myself. We really wanted to make a horror game with a unique setting, and to challenge ourselves to make a horror game that primarily takes place in the daylight.

I had a fantastic time working on this project, and as it stands it is only a game jam version, but I would love to expand the idea in the future into a full game.
