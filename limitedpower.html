Game Info

Limited Power is a wave defense game where you play as a soldier defending against waves of robots.

This game was made for the TriJam #228. I worked on this one solo, and it was a fun challenge to try and complete a game within such a short time frame of 3 hours. It taught me a lot about how to find the fun quickly, and not focus on the fluff too early.
