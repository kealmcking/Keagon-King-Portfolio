Game Info

Cosmic Golf is a 2D physics golf game where you play as a golf ball on various planets and moons. The game features a variety of hazards and obstacles that you can use to your advantage.

I am the sole developer and designer for the project, and it is currently in the beta testing phase. I started development on this game as an attempt to remake another title I had created for a game jam, called Gravity Golf. It was made in 8 hours, and had a fun, yet rough visual style. For Cosmic Golf, I wanted to try and create a more polished and polished experience, with a focus on creating a unique art style.

The game is still in development, but I am excited to share my progress so far! Additionally, a demo has been released on itch.io. Please give it a try!
