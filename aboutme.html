About Me

I am a longtime game enjoyer turned game developer. For my entire sentient existence I have wanted one thing for my career, and that is to make great video games. I have spent the last 6 years or more learning how to make that dream a reality. One way I love to work towards that goal is to do game jams. I believe they are a wonderful way to learn new skills, to meet new people, and to know how to work under extremely tight deadlines.

After many years of hobbyist experience, I decided to enroll in a degree program in order to fill the areas of my knowledge that I felt were lacking. Taking on this journey has taught me much, and introduced me to great people who I have had the pleasure to work with.

Now, in the last year of my degree program, I am looking to start my career in the industry in an internship role. I believe this would be an incredible opportunity to grow as a developer, and to make a real impact in the games industry.
