Game Info

Sender: Arbitration is a deduction story game where you play as a Sender, a being who decides the eternal fate of the recently deceased. You are tasked with questioning these souls in order to deduce their sins and virtues, and ultimately determine their fate.

This game was created for the Game Makers Toolkit Game Jam 2024, alongside a team who was entirely new to me. I was responsible for the UI design and implementation, as well as the overall UX of the game. It was a very fun experience, and I learned a lot about the importance of UX in a game, as well as working with new people.
